Old Exams

Although the general focus of the course—thermodynamics, equilibria, and kinetics—is fixed, the timing of exams and some topics vary from semester–to–semester. These old exams serve as a guide to how I construct exams and provide an opportunity to test yourself; if the context of a problem seems foreign to you, then it may be a topic we have not yet covered or a special topic that we will not cover this semester.
